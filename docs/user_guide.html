User Guide

Master your local network launchpad. Configuring, connecting, and controlling your PC remotely.

Getting Started

1

Launch Server

Navigate to the project folder and double-click start_aviator.bat.

Keep the terminal window open!

2

Add Apps

In the "WebApp Aviator Server" window, click Add Application... and select any .exe file.

Connecting Mobile

Instant QR Connection

The fastest way to connect. No typing required.

  1. Look at the top-right of the Server Window.
  2. Open your phone Camera.
  3. Scan the QR Code.
  4. Tap the link to open the Web App.

Scan Me

Requirement

Your Phone and PC must be connected to the Same Wi-Fi Network.

Advanced Features

Launch Arguments

Add flags like -windowed or --incognito by selecting an app and clicking Edit Args.

Remove Apps

Clean up your list by selecting an app and clicking Remove Selected.

Safe Stop

Click Stop Server or close the GUI to safely shut down all background processes.

Introduction

WebApp Aviator is a premium local network solution designed to bridge the gap between your mobile devices and your Windows desktop applications.

Launch Remotely

Trigger games, efficiency tools, or scripts on your PC directly from your phone's browser.

Zero Config

Automatic discovery via mDNS/Zeroconf. No IP typing required (just scan the QR code).

Premium UI

Glassmorphism design that looks native on iOS and Android devices.

Custom Arguments

Launch apps with specific flags (e.g. -bigpicture, --incognito).

Quick Start

1

Navigate to the project root.

2

Double click the start_aviator.bat script.

3

Add your favorite applications using the Desktop GUI.

4

Scan the QR code with your phone!
